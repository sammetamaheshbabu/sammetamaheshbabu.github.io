Privacy Policy

At Toor India, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with the utmost care and transparency. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

  • Information Collection: We collect communication information (such as phone number and email address), confirmation details (such as gender and age), and other optional details provided by you for a better service experience.
  • Use of Data: Your data is used solely for booking, communication, and service improvement. We do not use your data for marketing without your consent.
  • Third-Party Sharing: We only share your name and phone number with payment gateway partners for seamless transactions, and with verified travel/accommodation partners as required for your booking. We never sell your data.
  • Data Security: Your data is securely stored in reputable cloud services and protected against unauthorized access.
  • User Rights: You have full access to your data and may access, update, or delete it at any time via your account or by contacting us.
  • Cookies: We use cookies only for authentication and session management, not for advertising or tracking.
  • Review Images: With your consent, we may publish your review images on our social media accounts.

For any questions about your privacy, please contact us at contact@toorindia.com or support@toorindia.com.
